- Look for: A new method, algorithm, or system being proposed

- Example `implicit_structures`: ["Problem Definition", "Our Proposed Method", "Baseline Method"]
- Example `key_comparisons`: ["Our Method vs. QuickSort", "Performance vs. Accuracy"]

**For Literature Reviews / Position Papers:**
- Look for: Taxonomies or classifications of different approaches (e.g., "Type 1", "Type 2", "Type 3")
- Look for: Chronological progressions or historical phases (e.g., "The Early Era", "The Modern Era")
- Example `implicit_structures`: ["Taxonomy of Methods: [Statistical, Neural, Hybrid]"]
- Example `key_comparisons`: ["Author A's Position vs. Author B's Position"]
